5 Irresistible Chicken Pasta Recipes: Easy Dinners for Every Craving

Recipes 1/12/2026

Chicken and pasta – it's a match made in culinary heaven! This dynamic duo forms the basis for countless comforting, satisfying, and incredibly versatile meals. Whether you're looking for a quick weeknight solution or an impressive dish to serve guests, chicken pasta delivers every time. Its adaptability means you can go from rich and creamy to light and zesty, or even fiery and bold, all with just a few ingredient tweaks. Get ready to elevate your dinner game with our top 5 chicken pasta recipes that are guaranteed to become new family favorites.

First up, the ultimate crowd-pleaser: Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken Pasta. This dish is pure comfort in a bowl, featuring tender pan-seared chicken bathed in a luxurious sauce made from heavy cream, aromatic garlic, and nutty Parmesan cheese. It’s incredibly simple to prepare, yet tastes like something straight out of a gourmet Italian restaurant. Pair it with your favorite pasta shape – fettuccine or penne work beautifully – and watch it disappear faster than you can say "more, please!"

For those who love a little heat, our Spicy Cajun Chicken Pasta will ignite your taste buds. This vibrant dish combines succulent chicken with a medley of colorful bell peppers, onions, and a robust Cajun spice blend, all tossed in a zesty, tomato-based cream sauce. The kick from the Cajun seasoning is perfectly balanced by the richness of the cream, creating an exciting flavor profile that's both bold and incredibly addictive. Serve it with a long pasta like linguine to soak up all that incredible sauce.

If you're in the mood for something lighter and brighter, look no further than Lemon Herb Chicken Pasta. This refreshing recipe showcases pan-seared chicken breast tossed with a generous squeeze of fresh lemon juice, fragrant herbs like parsley and dill, and a light olive oil-based sauce. It’s a wonderfully fresh alternative to heavier pasta dishes, perfect for spring or summer evenings, or anytime you crave a burst of citrusy goodness. Add some cherry tomatoes or asparagus for extra color and nutrition.

Rounding out our top picks are two more stellar options. The Chicken Pesto Pasta offers a burst of fresh basil flavor, combining tender chicken with vibrant pesto (store-bought or homemade!), sun-dried tomatoes, and a sprinkle of pine nuts for texture. And for a truly hearty and classic option, our Chicken and Broccoli Alfredo combines creamy Alfredo sauce, tender chicken, and crisp-tender broccoli florets for a complete and satisfying meal that never disappoints. Each of these recipes offers a unique flavor profile, ensuring there's a chicken pasta dish for every craving and occasion.

These five chicken pasta recipes are more than just meals; they're an invitation to explore different flavors and techniques in your kitchen. They're proof that delicious, restaurant-quality food can be made right at home with minimal fuss. Don't be afraid to experiment with different herbs, vegetables, or even add a pinch of chili flakes for an extra kick. So grab your apron, pick your favorite recipe (or try all five!), and get ready to enjoy some of the best chicken pasta you've ever tasted. Buon appetito!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 lb fettuccine or penne pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese, plus more for garnish
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 cup fresh spinach (optional)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions until al dente. Drain and set aside, reserving 1/2 cup pasta water.
  2. While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken cubes and cook until golden brown and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove chicken from skillet and set aside.
  3. Add minced garlic to the same skillet and sauté for 1 minute until fragrant. Do not burn.
  4. Pour in chicken broth and heavy cream. Bring to a simmer, stirring constantly, and cook for 3-5 minutes until the sauce slightly thickens.
  5. Reduce heat to low. Stir in grated Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth. If using, stir in fresh spinach until wilted.
  6. Add the cooked chicken and drained pasta to the skillet with the sauce. Toss to coat everything evenly. If the sauce is too thick, add a tablespoon or two of the reserved pasta water until desired consistency is reached.
  7. Serve immediately, garnished with extra Parmesan cheese and fresh chopped parsley.
